About T3

A simple Phase 1 aggregator for local opportunity.

T3 maps employers within 25 miles of Tinicum Township, Bucks County, centered on 40.4825, -75.1069. It is built for job seekers, students, career changers, and local companies that want better visibility.

Local First

The directory focuses on Tinicum Township and surrounding communities, including ZIPs such as 18947 and nearby Bucks County towns.

Phase 1 Scope

This version lists companies, categories, websites, addresses, hiring status, employee counts, onboarding notes, and map locations. It does not include accounts, job posts, or a backend.
